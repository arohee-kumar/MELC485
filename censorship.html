Rise of Censorship

Include information about the media climate

There was a rise in censorship leading up to the Arab Spring. Egypt

  • January 25, 2011 - Twitter was blocked by State Security Investigation Service
  • January 26, 2011 - Facebook was shut down
  • January 27, 2011: night - Internet and SMS was blocked
People used back channels like WhatsApp and email platforms to communicate Egyptian Revolution
