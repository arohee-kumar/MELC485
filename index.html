Introduction

I want to explore how revolutions in the Middle East were impacted by media and media censorship. I will look at the Green Movement in Iran and the Arab Spring in Egypt in particular. I will talk about the background of what led to the Green Revolution and Arab Spring, censorship of social medias in the Middle East, and finally different social media mediums and how they are perceived.
